About CDU CODERS CLUB

The CDU coders club started its journey in 2012 with an initiative to bring the CDU’s IT professionals closer . We had an aim to provide support and share the best of skills amongst the community. Over the years the club had heavily diversified into activities of various sort including providing coaching by the industry leaders and arranging projects with the prime IT companies to brighten future career opportunities for our valued members. We also act as an IT company and an industry leader in the Northern Territory in providing excellent software’s and applications for our clienteles.
